CATL Annual Report 2024 — Supply Chain Disclosure

Contemporary Amperex Technology Co. Limited (CATL) is the world's largest
battery cell manufacturer, supplying lithium-ion battery cells to major
automakers globally.

Key supplier relationships:
- CATL sources cobalt primarily from mines in the Democratic Republic of
  the Congo, with Glencore as a major cobalt supplier under a long-term
  supply agreement.
- Lithium is sourced from operations in Chile through Albemarle Corporation
  and SQM under multi-year procurement contracts.
- CATL supplies battery cells to Tesla under a long-term supply agreement
  covering standard-range Model 3 and Model Y vehicles.
- CATL also supplies battery cells to BMW and Volkswagen for their
  electric vehicle platforms.
