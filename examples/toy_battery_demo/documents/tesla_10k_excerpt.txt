Tesla Inc. 10-K Annual Report — Supply Chain Risk Factors

Tesla depends on a limited number of suppliers for battery cells, a critical
component of all Tesla vehicles. CATL and LG Energy Solution are Tesla's
primary battery cell suppliers. A disruption at either supplier could
materially impact vehicle production.

Tesla sources lithium for its Nevada operations from Albemarle Corporation
under a multi-year supply agreement. Nickel is sourced from BHP under a
supply contract for battery-grade nickel sulfate.
